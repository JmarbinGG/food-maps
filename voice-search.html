How to Use Smart Search

1

Open the Search Interface

Click the button below or the floating icon at bottom-right. You'll see either a microphone (voice) or text input (type).

2

Ask Your Question

Speak or type your question naturally. Examples: "Where can I get produce near me?" or "Show me vegetarian options."

3

Get Results

The system will process your request and show you the results. With voice support, you'll also hear responses.

4

Continue Searching

Ask follow-up questions or start a new search. Your conversation history is saved.

Works in All Browsers

Voice Input Available:

  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari (iOS/macOS)

Text Input Always Works:

  • Firefox
  • All other browsers
  • Same smart search features

Tip: For voice input, allow microphone access when prompted by your browser.
