# 📌 Quick Start: Bookmark Feature

## How to Bookmark Food Listings

### Step 1: Find a Listing
Browse the map or list view to find food you're interested in.

### Step 2: Click the Star
```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│                      [☆]    │  ← Click this star!
│  Fresh Vegetables           │
│  10 lbs of mixed veggies    │
│  📍 123 Main St             │
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

### Step 3: Star Turns Gold
```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│                      [⭐]    │  ← Now it's bookmarked!
│  Fresh Vegetables           │
│  10 lbs of mixed veggies    │
│  📍 123 Main St             │
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

### Step 4: View Your Bookmarks
1. Click Profile Menu (top right)
2. Select "⭐ My Favorites"
3. Click "🍎 Food Listings" tab

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────┐
│  ⭐ My Favorites               [✕]   │
├──────────────────────────────────────┤
│  [📍 Locations] [🍎 Food Listings]   │  ← Click this tab
├──────────────────────────────────────┤
│  🍎 Fresh Vegetables       [🗑️]      │
│  10 lbs of mixed vegetables          │
│  📍 123 Main St  📦 10 lbs          │
│  Status: Available                   │
└──────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Why Use Bookmarks?

✅ **Save for Later** - Bookmark items you want to claim when ready
✅ **Compare Options** - Save multiple listings to compare
✅ **Track Favorites** - Remember trusted food sources
✅ **Plan Ahead** - Build your weekly food pickup routine
✅ **Quick Access** - All your favorites in one place

## Tips

💡 **Bookmark Multiple Items**
   Save 3-4 options, then pick the best one

💡 **Check Regularly**
   Bookmarked items may get claimed by others

💡 **Remove Old Bookmarks**
   Keep your list fresh and relevant

💡 **Use with Locations**
   Combine bookmarked listings with saved locations

## Common Questions

**Q: Do bookmarks expire?**
A: No, but the listing itself may expire or get claimed.

**Q: Can others see my bookmarks?**
A: No, bookmarks are private to your account.

**Q: How many can I bookmark?**
A: Unlimited! Bookmark as many as you want.

**Q: Do bookmarks work offline?**
A: Yes, they're saved on your device.

## Quick Actions

| Action | How To |
|--------|--------|
| Bookmark | Click ☆ star on listing |
| Unbookmark | Click ⭐ star again |
| View All | Profile → My Favorites → Food Listings |
| Remove | Click 🗑️ in Favorites panel |
