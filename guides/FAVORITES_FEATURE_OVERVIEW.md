## 📱 How to Use

1. **Access**: Profile Menu → "⭐ My Favorites"
2. **Add**: Click "+ Add New Favorite Location"
3. **Fill**: Enter name, address, and notes
4. **Save**: Click "💾 Save Favorite"
5. **View**: See all your saved locations
6. **Remove**: Click 🗑️ to delete
