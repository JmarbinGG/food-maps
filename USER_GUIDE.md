## 📅 Donation Scheduler

**For Donors: Schedule Recurring Food Donations**

### How to Access
1. Click on your profile menu (top right)
2. Select **"📅 Donation Scheduler"**

### Creating a Schedule

1. **Click "Create New Schedule"**
2. **Fill in the details:**
   - **Title**: Name your schedule (e.g., "Weekly Bakery Donations")
   - **Category**: Select food type (Produce, Prepared, Bakery, etc.)
   - **Quantity & Unit**: How much you'll donate (e.g., 50 lbs)
   - **Frequency**: Choose how often
     - **Daily** - Every day
     - **Weekly** - Pick a day of the week
     - **Biweekly** - Every 2 weeks on chosen day
     - **Monthly** - Pick day of month (1-31)
     - **Custom** - Set your own interval in days

3. **Set Reminder Preferences:**
   - ✅ Enable reminders to get notified before donations
   - Choose how many hours before (default: 24 hours)

4. **Click "Create Schedule"**

### Managing Your Schedules

**Pause/Resume:**
- Click the pause button (⏸️) to temporarily stop a schedule
- Click resume (▶️) to reactivate it

**Edit:**
- Click "Edit" to modify any schedule details
- Changes will recalculate the next donation date

**Delete:**
- Click "Delete" to permanently remove a schedule

**View Reminders:**
- Switch to the "Reminders" tab
- See all upcoming donation reminders
- **Dismiss** - Hide reminder without action
- **Mark Complete** - Record donation and schedule next one

## ⭐ Favorites & Saved Locations

**Bookmark Trusted Spots for Quick Access**

### How to Access
1. Click on your profile menu (top right)
2. Select **"⭐ My Favorites"**

### Why Use Favorites?

**Perfect for Families with Routines:**
- Save locations you visit regularly
- Quick access to trusted food sources
- Remember helpful details about each spot
- Never forget where the best donations are

### Adding a Favorite Location

1. **Click "+ Add New Favorite Location"** (in Locations tab)
2. **Fill in the details:**
   - **Name**: Give it a memorable name (e.g., "Community Food Bank")
   - **Address**: Full address of the location **(Required)**
   - **Latitude/Longitude**: Optional GPS coordinates for precise mapping
   - **Notes**: Add helpful reminders (e.g., "Open Tuesdays 9-5, parking in back")

3. **Click "💾 Save Favorite"**

### Bookmarking Food Listings

1. **Find a listing** you're interested in
2. **Click the ☆ star icon** in the top-right corner of the listing card
3. **Star turns gold** (⭐) when bookmarked
4. **View all bookmarked listings** in Favorites → Food Listings tab
5. **Click star again** to remove bookmark

### Managing Your Favorites

**Two Types of Favorites:**

1. **Locations Tab** - Saved addresses and places
   - Food banks
   - Distribution centers
   - Regular pickup spots
   - Any address you want to remember

2. **Food Listings Tab** - Bookmarked food donations
   - Specific food items you're interested in
   - Donations you want to claim later
   - Items you're tracking

**View All Saved Locations:**
- See all your bookmarked spots in one place
- Each location shows:
  - Name and address
  - Your personal notes
  - Date added
  - GPS status (if coordinates saved)

**Remove a Favorite:**
- Click the 🗑️ trash icon
- Confirm removal
- Location is removed from your favorites

### Use Cases

**For Recipients:**
- Save your regular pickup locations
- **Bookmark interesting food listings** to claim later
- Remember which food banks have the best hours
- Note which places have parking
- Track locations with specific dietary options
- **Keep an eye on specific donors** you trust

**For Donors:**
- Bookmark your favorite distribution centers
- Save addresses of regular recipients
- Remember drop-off instructions
- Keep track of community partners
- **Monitor popular listings** for insights

**For Families:**
- Create a routine of trusted locations
- **Bookmark regular food sources** for quick access
- Share location details with family members
- Plan weekly food pickup routes
- Remember special instructions for each spot
- **Track favorite food items** that appear regularly

### Pro Tips

💡 **Add Detailed Notes:**
- Include operating hours
- Note parking availability
- Mention contact person names
- Record any special requirements

💡 **Use GPS Coordinates:**
- Helps with precise navigation
- Useful for hard-to-find locations
- Better for rural areas

💡 **Keep It Updated:**
- Remove locations you no longer visit
- Update notes if hours change
- Add new favorites as you discover them

## 🆘 Getting Help

### Support Options

**In-App Support:**
1. Profile Menu → **"Messages & Support"**
2. Send message to support team
3. Get response within 24 hours

**Safety Issues:**
1. Go to **Safety & Trust Center**
2. Click **"Report"** tab
3. Submit safety concern

**Email Support:**
- noreply.foodmaps@gmail.com

### FAQ

**Q: How do I increase my trust score?**
A: Complete verifications, show up on time, get positive feedback, and be responsive.

**Q: Can I cancel a scheduled donation?**
A: Yes, either pause the schedule or delete it entirely from the Donation Scheduler.

**Q: What if someone doesn't show up?**
A: Report it in the Safety & Trust Center under the Report tab.

**Q: How do I verify my email?**
A: Go to Safety & Trust Center → Verification tab → Click "Verify" for email.

**Q: Can I schedule one-time donations?**
A: Use the regular listing creation. Schedules are for recurring donations only.

**Q: How do I save a favorite location?**
A: Go to Profile Menu → "⭐ My Favorites" → Click "Add New Favorite Location" and enter the details.

**Q: Can I add notes to my saved locations?**
A: Yes! When adding or editing a favorite, you can include notes like hours, parking info, or special instructions.
