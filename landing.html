How Food Maps Works

Food sharing in three clear steps

1. Share Food

Donors post available food in about a minute.

2. Match Nearby

The system matches food by distance, dietary needs, and urgency.

3. Deliver and Track

Volunteers get route details, and recipients get status updates.
