How Food Maps Works

Simple, fast, and intelligent food distribution in three steps

1. Share Food

Donors upload available food in 60 seconds. Our AI validates, geocodes, and estimates nutritional value automatically.

2. AI Matching

Multi-agent system analyzes proximity, dietary needs, household size, and urgency to create optimal matches.

3. Smart Delivery

Volunteers receive optimized routes while recipients get real-time updates and delivery tracking.
