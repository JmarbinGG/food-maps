Voice Search

Quick Reference Guide

How to Start

1. Tap the purple microphone button
2. Wait for the pulsing rings
3. Speak your question clearly
4. Listen to the response!

Try Saying:

"Where can I get produce near me?"
"Is anything open after 6?"
"Do you have vegetarian food?"
"Find food without nuts"
"Within 2 miles"

What You Can Ask

Find by Location

  • • "Near me"
  • • "Nearby"
  • • "Close to me"
  • • "Within [X] miles"

Find by Time

  • • "Open now"
  • • "After 6 PM"
  • • "Tonight"
  • • "Before noon"

Dietary Needs

  • • "Vegetarian"
  • • "Vegan"
  • • "Halal"
  • • "Gluten-free"

Tips for Success

DO:

  • Speak clearly and naturally
  • Wait for the pulsing rings
  • Ask in your own words
  • Be specific about what you need

DON'T:

  • Speak too fast
  • Use technical jargon
  • Cover the microphone
  • Worry about perfect grammar

Simple Commands

"Find food near me"
"What's open now?"
"Help me"

Having Trouble?

Q:
Microphone not working?

Check your browser settings and allow microphone access.

Q:
Not understanding you?

Try speaking slower and clearer. Click the help button for examples.

Q:
No sound response?

Check your volume. You can toggle voice responses with the audio button.
